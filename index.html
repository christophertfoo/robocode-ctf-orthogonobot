Welcome to the robocode-ctf-orthogonobot project.

robocode-ctf-orthogonobot is a Robocode robot that attempts crush its opponents with a mix of offense and defense.

Offense

  • Uses a distance sensitive firing mechanism to ensure that the strongest (and most costly) bullets are saved only for close range targets that are more likely to be hit.
  • If the enemy is almost dead and orthogonobot is still healthy, go in for the kill! Chase the enemy down and fire the strongest bullets from point-blank range.

Defense

  • Recognizes when an opponent fires a bullet and dodges out of the way using a location sensitive methods to prevent the orthogonobot from becoming cornered.
  • Keep the orthogonobot's body perpendicular to the enemy. The quickest path out of a bullet's path is a line perpendicular to its trajectory.

Weaknesses

  • Currently fires directly at the enemy with no leading. Accuracy against constantly moving robots can be poor.
